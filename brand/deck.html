The problem

Most agents happily invent a number

The smarter ones add an auditor that checks whether the AI was honest about its number.

That still passes a verdict that is wrong as long as it looks honest. In a high-stakes financial workflow that is a liability, not a quirk.

High-stakes posture

Refusal-first & human sign-off

  • The desk screens and audits only. It does not buy, sell, or act.
  • Every round ends AWAITING HUMAN SIGN-OFF — manusia memutuskan.
  • Brain is swappable by one env var. The decision does not live in the model.
